About Me

👤 Get to Know Me

Hello! I’m Haziq Danial, a curious soul with a passion for history, music, films, and solving Rubik’s cubes. I find joy in discovering the stories behind past events, immersing myself in melodies, and getting lost in cinematic journeys.

Whether I’m untangling the timelines of historical wars, vibing to a new track, or twisting a cube in record time, I value depth, meaning, and moments that challenge my thinking. My interests make me both creative and analytical — and I enjoy that balance.

I believe learning never ends, and every hobby or film I enjoy helps shape how I view the world. I’m someone who appreciates both structure and spontaneity.

🎯 My Personal Values & Goals

  • Curiosity: I always seek to learn something new.
  • Creativity: Whether it's music or problem-solving, I enjoy expressing ideas uniquely.
  • Discipline: I strive to stay consistent in my passions and responsibilities.
  • Future Goal: To pursue a fulfilling career that blends my love for knowledge, storytelling, and creativity.
