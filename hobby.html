🎲 Solving the Rubik’s Cube

One of my favorite hobbies is solving the Rubik’s Cube. It’s more than just twisting a puzzle — it’s about pattern recognition, muscle memory, and logical problem-solving. The sense of accomplishment after completing a solve never gets old.

📜 Learning & Competing

I enjoy learning new solving methods, optimizing my speed, and watching competitions. It’s a great way to engage both the brain and hands while being part of a creative community.

My Cubing Stats

  • Average 3x3 Time: ~18 seconds
  • Fastest Solve: 12.8 seconds
  • Preferred Method: CFOP
  • Other Cubes: 2x2, 4x4, Pyraminx

Why I Love It

  • Improves focus and concentration
  • Helps me relax and stay productive
  • It’s a skill that feels rewarding
